ABOUT OUR FOUNDATION

Blessed Foundation, founded on August 12, 2024 by Dr. Emmanuel Steven Kamara, is a Non-Governmental Organization dedicated to transforming lives through:

  • Elevating and empowering youth through innovative development programs
  • Combating child malnutrition and poverty through sustainable solutions
  • Creating awareness on HIV/AIDS and mental health through effective communication
